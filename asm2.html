Some Assembly notes:

I've seen this as assembly and assembler. I think of assembler as the compiler and assembly as the language.

An example makefile:

			#makefile for hello.asm
			hello: hello.o
				ld -o hello hello.o
			hello.o: hello.asm
				nasm -f elf64 hello.asm

A comment is preceeded with a semi colon ; .
